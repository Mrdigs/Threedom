Compound Animations

The components of compound objects should all be able to be animated independently, like moving parts of a machine.

Single Plane

These two cylinders are sat on each face of a rotating 2 face object, hence they are facing in opposite directions. Each of them is set to be spinning, so they should spin individually while also rotating with the parent object. Note they are spinning in opposite directions because of their distinct orientation.
